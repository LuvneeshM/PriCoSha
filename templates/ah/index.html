Steammed Cabbage with Tofu Pepper Filling

Ingredients:

  • Cabbage
  • Salt(To taste)
  • 1 box of tofu
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Table Spoon of Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 Cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup of Honey Sauce
  • 1 or 2 Peppers (any kind, i.e Bell Pepper of Fresno)

Directions:

  1. Set a pan of water with salt to boil. Take 1 sheet of cabbage and boil in the salt water until soft.
  2. Crush 1 box of Tofu and chop peppers.
  3. Cut and dice 1 onion into small pieces.
  4. Saute tofu, chop peppers, and onion in pan with 1 table spoon of sesame oil.
  5. Add 0.5 cup of Soy Sauce to the pan and mix.
  6. Stuff filling into cabbage and steam for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve with soy sauce and honey sauce.

Vietnamese Salad Rolls

Ingrediants:

  • Red Lettuce
  • Salt(To taste)
  • 1 lb. Rice stick noodles
  • 1 lb. Carrots, peeled, finely julienned
  • Lime Juice
  • Shrimp
  • 1 bunch of Cilantro leaves
  • 1 bunch of Mint leaves
  • 1 bunch of thai basil leaves
  • 1/2 Cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup of Honey Sauce

Directions:

  1. Cook rice noodle for 3 minutes in boiling water. Take out, shock and drain.
  2. Blanch shrimp with shell on in 160ยบ water until done. Shock and peel the shells off. Slice shrimp lengthwise in half and devein.
  3. Marinate julienne carrots with salt for 10 minutes, squeeze out excess liquid. Add lime juice to drained carrots.
  4. Set a pan of water with salt to boil. Take 1 sheet of cabbage and boil in the salt water until soft.
  5. Stuff shrimp, carrots, cilantro leaves, mint leaves into cabbage and steam for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with thai basil leaves on top and with soy sauce and honey sauce on side.
